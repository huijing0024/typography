SF Whale Tour

FIELD
Typography, poster design
INFO
A Series of SF whale tour posters.

projects.

This project is about redesign posters of SF Whale Tour. The posters at before was boring and no images on the posters. I did a lot of research before I design. In this series poster, I tried to use hand writing typeface in the poster. There are three parts of my posters. There are same concept, but different three ideas in my posters. The first one is "Sit on the Boat, and Enjoy the tour".
The second poster is talking about "Feel the Spray". I used the image about whale which in the sea. The topic in this poster is to show audience that they can feel the spray by taking the SF Whale Tour. I was trying to use hand writing typeface online, but I realized those fonts are not really nature, which means they are same like hand writing but actually not. Finally, I used typeface that I write by myself. For now, they are much better.
The last poster is talking about the "Explore Secrets of the Farallon Island", which is my third idea that to show audiences what can you get form the whale tour. The three poster are completely shows three benefits of SF Whale Tour. Later, I will do the web design and brochure design for SF Whale Tour to complete my whole idea.
