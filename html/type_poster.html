A Series of Type classification posters.

projects.

The first poster is talking about the “Garalde”, which is one of the type classifications. In this poster, I choose “hello” as my focus point, and figured out which part that is different with other typefaces.
The second poster is talking about the “Didone”, which is one of the type classifications. The focus point is “hey”, which in the center of this poster. I used same idea of the first one. “Hello” and “hey” are same meaning in English, but it also has a little bit different. That is the same idea with my concept. Those three posters are all serif typefaces, but they are different at details.
The last poster is talking about the “Transitional”, which is also one of the type classifications. The main part is “hi”, which has similar meaning with “hello” and “hey”, but still different in details. The biggest challenge is colors in those three posters, it is hard to combine different colors together and make it looks like a visual system.
